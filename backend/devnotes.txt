Testing:
- view all users: curl http://localhost:8080/api/v1/users
- (valid) add new user: curl http://localhost:8080/api/v1/users -X POST -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"username":"admin","password":"admin123","full_name":"Thanos"}'
- (invalid) add new user: curl http://localhost:8080/api/v1/users -X POST -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{}'
